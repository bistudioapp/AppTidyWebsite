Why not just drag apps to Trash?

Dragging an app bundle to Trash removes the app itself, but preferences, caches, containers, receipts, and support folders can remain in your Library. AppTidy helps you review those related files before cleanup.

Review-first by design

Recommended items are separated from review items. AppTidy explains matches and keeps cleanup deliberate instead of silently deleting everything that looks similar.

How AppTidy uninstalls a Mac app

Choose or drop an app

Drop an app onto AppTidy or choose one from the installed app list.

Review related files

See preferences, caches, containers, logs, receipts, and support files with matching reasons.

Move selected items to Trash

AppTidy moves selected files to Trash, not permanent deletion, so Finder can still restore them.

Good fit for careful Mac cleanup

AppTidy is best for people who want a focused Mac uninstaller rather than a broad booster, antivirus, or one-click system cleaner. It stays close to the actual uninstall task and makes review visible.
