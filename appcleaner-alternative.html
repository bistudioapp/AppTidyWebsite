Alternative Guide

A review-first AppCleaner alternative for modern Mac cleanup.

AppTidy keeps the familiar app-uninstaller idea, then emphasizes explicit review, leftover tools, Homebrew awareness, and local cleanup history.

AppCleaner is a product of its respective owner. This page is a factual comparison for people researching Mac uninstall tools, not an affiliation or endorsement claim.

Why consider AppTidy?

Some Mac users want a cleaner with more visible review states and newer workflow surfaces. AppTidy is built around showing why files match, keeping uncertain items review-only, and moving selected items to Trash.

Comparison focus

Workflow Classic uninstall tools often focus on drag-and-drop cleanup. AppTidy adds review-first explanations and local history.
Leftover review Leftover handling varies by tool and rule set. AppTidy has a dedicated Leftovers surface for orphaned files.
Removal behavior Users should always check how a cleaner deletes files. AppTidy moves selected items to Trash, not permanent deletion in the current release.
Integrations Many uninstallers focus on app bundle cleanup. AppTidy can detect Homebrew casks and offer zap command guidance.
