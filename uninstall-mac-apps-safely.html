Safe Uninstall

Uninstall Mac apps with review before removal.

AppTidy is built around a simple safety rule: show the related files first, then move only selected items to Trash.

Trash-first cleanup

AppTidy moves selected files to Trash instead of permanently deleting them. You can restore files from Finder if you change your mind.

Signed and notarized

Public AppTidy releases are signed and notarized for macOS, with a checksum endpoint available for download verification.

A safer app cleanup habit

A good uninstaller should not hide decisions. AppTidy shows matching reasons, avoids permanent deletion in the current release, and keeps higher-risk review items separate from stronger recommendations.
