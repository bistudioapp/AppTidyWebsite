Privacy

Privacy Policy

AppTidy is built to work locally on your Mac by default. It does not require a cloud account to scan, review, or move files to Trash.

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Local App Data

AppTidy scans file paths, app bundle metadata, sizes, and related cleanup evidence on your Mac. This data is used to show scan results and cleanup history inside the app.

No Cloud Account Required

AppTidy does not require sign-in for the core uninstall flow. Scan results and cleanup history are stored locally on your Mac. AppTidy does not collect analytics or diagnostics.

Full Disk Access

Full Disk Access lets AppTidy inspect protected user data locations when you grant permission in macOS System Settings. It does not give AppTidy a cloud connection and it does not bypass administrator approval for root-owned apps.

Website Logs

Like most websites, app-tidy.com may receive standard hosting logs such as IP address, browser type, referring page, and request time. These logs are used for security, delivery, and basic site reliability.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the AppTidy team through the contact details published on app-tidy.com.
